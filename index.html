Projects

Job Satisfaction, Management Sentiment, and Financial Performance: Text Analysis with Job Reviews

  • Scraped 1.2 mil+ job ratings and review texts of publicly listed companies and merged with financial performance data from Compustat for 2014-2018
  • Trained numerous supervised ML algorithms to predict job sentiment with review text. Highest accuracy of 88% achieved with XGBoost and deep neural nets.
  • On average, a 1% increase in job ratings increases a company's market value by approximately 0.7%, revenue by 0.6-1%.
    • Established novel evidence that sentiment towards a company management has a non-linear effect on financial performance

Paper, Code

The Proximity of Ideas: An Analysis of Patent Text Using Machine Learning

  • Converted 1.4 mil+ patent texts into meaningful numerical data using ML/NLP algorithms Doc2Vec and topic models (Latent Dirichlet Allocation)
  • Using my machine learning method, I find that patents from the same city are 0-0.06 times more similar than patents from different cities. This shows that urban agglomeration has a small effect on flow of innovative ideas
    • Different to prior research: large role for urban agglomeration since patents from the same city are 1-2 times more likely to be cited than patents from different cities

Paper, Code, Presentation

Strategic Citations in Patents Using Machine Learning

  • Designed data experiments to find novel evidence of strategic bias in patent citation patterns using NLP text similarity.
    • Citations to textually similar patents in different cities are much less likely than similar patents in the same city
    • Inventors cite their own prior patents less after they change firms
